What's New?

The Samsung Galaxy S25 is the new series of high-end Android-based smartphones developed and marketed by Samsung as part of its Galaxy S Series. They collectively serve as the successor to the Galaxy S24 series. The S25, S25+ and S25 Ultra models were announced on January 22, 2025, at the Galaxy Unpacked event in San Jose, California, and were released on February 7, 2025.
